## Firm Overview

The Law Office of Jessica Bernstein serves clients throughout Travis, Williamson, and Hays Counties. Led by Jessica Bernstein—a distinguished graduate of the University of Texas School of Law and a former public defender trainer—the firm is built on a foundation of zealous advocacy, deep institutional knowledge of local court systems, and an unyielding commitment to defending the rights of the accused. 

Whether navigating the complexities of a first-time DWI or presenting a comprehensive mitigation packet to a Travis County grand jury for a felony charge, our mission remains absolute: delivering honest advice, compassionate support, and a dedicated defense to secure the best possible outcome.

## Core Practice Areas

### 24-Hour Jail Release
Immediate, round-the-clock defense advocacy. By utilizing attorney bonds, we bypass the standard bureaucratic delays of the Travis County Jail system, securing releases hours faster than traditional channels.

### DWI Defense
A comprehensive, scientific approach to combating driving under the influence charges. From challenging illegal traffic stops to auditing chemical breath tests and blood search warrants, we mount a rigorous defense to protect your record and your driver's license.

### Traffic Tickets
Minimizing the impact of traffic citations on your record, insurance premiums, and commercial driving privileges. We represent clients at municipal and justice of the peace courts to seek dismissals and deferrals.

### Felonies & Misdemeanors
Aggressive representation across the full spectrum of Texas criminal allegations. We draft detailed mitigation packets for pre-indictment grand jury reviews and build battle-tested trial defense strategies.
