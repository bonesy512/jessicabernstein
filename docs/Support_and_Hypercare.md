# Support & Post-Launch Hypercare Protocol
**Law Office of Jessica Bernstein**

At Bonesy Design, we stand by the quality of our work. To ensure a seamless transition and absolute stability, your website launch is protected by our official **Post-Launch Hypercare Period**.

---

## 1. Post-Launch Hypercare Overview

### What is Hypercare?
Hypercare is a **30-day dedicated monitoring and support period** starting immediately from the date of the live production deployment. During this window, our primary objective is to guarantee the security, stability, and integrity of your new website.

### Included Scope:
* **Critical Bug Resolution:** Swift fixes for any rendering bugs, layout breaks, or functional defects (e.g., button failures, form submission errors).
* **Performance Monitoring:** Continuous monitoring of Vercel edge performance, search engine accessibility, and page load speeds.
* **Security Tracking:** Verification of SSL certificates, security headers, and domain routing stability.
* **Integration Checks:** Ensuring third-party widgets (Avvo, Google Reviews, LawPay) load correctly without console errors or visual degradation.

*Note: Hypercare is limited to verifying, maintaining, and fixing the agreed-upon scope of the launch. New feature requests, content writing, design changes, or addition of new practice areas are outside the scope of Hypercare and will be handled via standard design/development hourly iterations.*

## 2. Service Level Agreements (SLAs) & Response Times

We categorize issues to ensure that high-impact problems are resolved with the urgency they require.

| Severity Level | Definition | Target Response Time | Target Resolution Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Severity 1 (Critical)** | Core website is down; contact forms fail to load/submit; the 24-hour jail release system is unresponsive. | **Within 2 Hours** | **Within 6 Hours** |
| **Severity 2 (High)** | A major page is broken or unreadable; buttons do not click; third-party payment portals route incorrectly. | **Within 4 Hours** | **Within 12 Hours** |
| **Severity 3 (Medium)** | Minor styling issues; text errors; Avvo or Google widgets fail to render but the page is otherwise functional. | **Within 24 Hours** | **Within 48 Hours** |
| **Severity 4 (Low)** | Minor typos; request for access assistance; small adjustments to image sizes or alignments. | **Within 48 Hours** | **Within 72 Hours** |

## 3. How to Submit a Support Ticket

To ensure your request is tracked, processed, and routed to the correct engineer immediately, please follow our support submission protocol:

### Step 1: Open a Ticket
Send an email to our support desk:
* **Support Email:** `support@bonesydesign.com`
* **Subject Format:** `[SUPPORT] Law Office of Jessica Bernstein - [Brief Issue Summary]`

### Step 2: Provide Context Details
In your email body, please include:
1. **The Page URL:** The specific web page where the issue occurs (e.g., `/contact` or `/practice-areas/dwi`).
2. **Device / Browser:** The device you are using (e.g., iPhone 15, Windows Laptop) and the browser (e.g., Safari, Chrome).
3. **Step-by-Step Description:** Exactly what you clicked and what happened.
4. **Screenshots or Videos:** A visual capture of the error (if possible).

## 4. Transition to Standard Maintenance

At the end of the 30-day Hypercare window, your website will transition to standard operational status. We will provide options for ongoing hosting maintenance, backup management, and monthly performance reports to ensure your site continues to run flawlessly.
