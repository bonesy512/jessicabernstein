# Standard Operating Procedure: Client Acquisition & Project Scoping

**Document ID:** S&CO-SOP-PRE-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** Business Development

## 1.0 Project Overview & Purpose
This formalizes the pre-engagement process for all new business opportunities. The purpose is to systematically convert qualified leads into signed clients by conducting thorough due diligence, defining precise project parameters, and establishing mutual strategic alignment.

## 2.0 Objectives
* **Qualify:** Evaluate inbound leads using the BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) framework to ensure they align with our strategic and financial criteria.
* **Analyze:** Conduct in-depth discovery to comprehend a prospective client's business goals, technical requirements, and success metrics.
* **Architect:** Design a high-level strategic and technical solution that addresses client needs.

## 3.0 Procedure
1.  **Lead Evaluation:** Assist Business Development team in evaluating leads. Use BANT framework.
2.  **Discovery Call:** Schedule and conduct discovery sessions with the prospective client.
3.  **Internal Scoping:** Determine technical requirements and timeline estimates based on discovery.
4.  **Proposal & SOW Generation:** Draft comprehensive client-facing Proposals and Statements of Work (SOWs) outlining project background, deliverables, timelines, and costs.

## 4.0 Key Deliverables
* **Phase 1:** Lead Qualification Scorecard, Go/No-Go Decision Log
* **Phase 2:** Detailed Discovery Call Notes & Action Items, Internal Debrief Summary
* **Phase 3:** Internal Technical Scoping Document, High-Level Resource & Timeline Estimate
* **Phase 4:** Client-Facing Proposal Document, Draft Client-Facing Statement of Work
* **Phase 5:** Signed Statement of Work & Master Service Agreement

## 5.0 Assumptions & Exclusions
* Leads are generated through existing channels. Key client stakeholders will be available.
* This SOW does not cover project execution. No code will be written during this phase.

## 6.0 Acceptance Criteria
Considered complete upon mutual execution (signing) of a client-facing SOW and Master Service Agreement (MSA).
