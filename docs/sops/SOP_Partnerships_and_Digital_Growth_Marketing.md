# Standard Operating Procedure: Partnerships & Digital Growth Marketing

**Document ID:** S&CO-SOP-004
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** Growth Strategy

## 1.0 Purpose
To establish a systematic, repeatable framework for delivering post-launch growth services. Converts digital assets into engines for measurable business growth through digital marketing campaigns and strategic alliances.

## 2.0 Scope
Applies to all post-launch, retainer-based client engagements focused on growth (either built into initial SOW or upsold).

## 3.0 Roles & Responsibilities
* **Growth Strategist:** Strategic advisor, develops Growth Blueprint, accountable for KPIs.
* **Partnership Lead:** Identifies and manages potential partners.

## 4.0 Procedure & Core Deliverables
1.  **Quarterly Growth Marketing Blueprints:** Assist in drafting blueprints and defining OKRs (Objectives and Key Results).
2.  **Partnership Briefs & Pipelines:** Evaluate and manage potential partners (Strategic fit, audience overlap).
3.  **Content Calendars:** Map out content production (Publish Date, Title, Pillar, Format, Keywords).
4.  **Monthly Performance Reports:** Generate templates for data-driven updates (Executive Summary, Performance vs KPIs, Channel Deep Dive).
5.  **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs):** Structured slide outlines for client leadership presentations to demonstrate value and set next quarter's stage.

## 5.0 Operational Guidelines
* Ensure all generated communication emphasizes transparency.
* Maintain regular cadence for KPI tracking.
* Request specific client details before generating performance templates to ensure data accuracy.
