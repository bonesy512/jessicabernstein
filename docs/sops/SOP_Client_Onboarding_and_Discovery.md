# Standard Operating Procedure: Client Onboarding & Discovery

**Document ID:** S&CO-SOP-002
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** Project Management

## 1.0 Purpose
To establish a standardized, efficient, and thorough process for onboarding new clients and conducting the initial discovery phase. Ensures every project begins with a clear understanding of goals and expectations.

## 2.0 Scope
Applies from the moment an SOW is signed until the project moves into the Execution Phase. Covers initial client communications, information gathering, and the official kickoff.

## 3.0 Roles & Responsibilities
* **Project Lead:** Primary internal point of contact.
* **Client POC:** Primary stakeholder on the client side.
* **Team Members:** Contribute to discovery documentation.

## 4.0 Procedure
* **Step 1: Project Initiation (Internal Setup):** Triggered by receipt of signed SOW. Draft internal Project Blueprints and high-level Project Roadmaps.
* **Step 2: Client Welcome & Information Gathering:** Generate customized onboarding packages, including Welcome Letters, Access Request Forms (secure password manager required), and Strategic Discovery Questionnaires.
* **Step 3: Kickoff Preparation:** Prepare timed Project Kickoff Agendas and structure Communication Plans outlining meeting rhythms and key contacts. Ensure transparency and clarity over speed.
* **Step 4: Kickoff Meeting:** Execute the kickoff agenda.
* **Step 5: Post-Kickoff Alignment:** Finalize Blueprint based on kickoff discoveries.

## 5.0 Core Artifacts
* **Project Blueprint:** The strategic compass.
* **Project Roadmap:** High-level project timeline (Phase 0 through Phase 5).
* **Project Kickoff Agenda:** Timed meeting structure (Introductions, Vision, Discovery Q&A, Roadmap, Ways of Working).
* **Communication Plan:** Defines channels (Slack, Email, Meetings) and Response Time SLAs.
